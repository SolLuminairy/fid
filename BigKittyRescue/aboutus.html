We are a local cat rescue that partners with local shelters to find strays a loving and safe home. We rescuse local strays and bring them to facilities in our area to help get them the care and love they need. We bring together local adoption facilities and put up a some of their availablity lists on our site to help direct you to their sites and find the furry friend of your dreams in just one site and to help you find our partners so we can all do good work together!
